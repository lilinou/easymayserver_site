Who We Are

Based out of the company’s headquarters on New York’s storied Wall Street location, Easymay Group is committed to delivering students premium resources and opportunities to further their education goals. Easymay Education and Easymay Live represent high-end sub-brands in the sectors of education and US real estate service respectively. The company’s distinction originates from its bespoke high-quality service, results-oriented focus, vast overseas resources, and the employment of a team of US-based professionals with unparalleled pedigree. Easymay Group's solutions range from long-term education planning, study abroad applications, vocational training, study roadmaps, and plans, exam preparation, and real estate consultation, amongst other customized solutions. Easymay has garnered high levels of credibility and transform into a leading educational consulting brand in the US.

In 2018, Easymay Group completed strategic investments from institutional sponsor based in China, E-House (China) Holdings Limited, amounting to over Tens of millions of dollars. In 2016 and 2017, Easymay was the only U.S. based educational consulting company in “North America University Tour”, and was the exclusive sponsor for “US-China VC Summit & Startup Expo” in education sector. The education group collaborates with a proprietary network that encompasses over 60 top universities and more than 70,000 international students in the US. Easymay is the only educational consulting company that passed the background investigation to participate major summits in Ivy Leagues and other top tier universities such as Harvard University, University of Pennsylvania, Columbia University, and Cornell University, and have correspondence with admission professionals. Easymay’s exclusive pool with over 50 top 30 ranked US universities former school admissions officers, and thousands of senior executives from leading corporate enterprises, enabled the students to have one-on-one private consultation. By combining the top education resources and sophisticated consulting service team from Easymay, students are given a strong chance to achieve their dream schools and dream companies.
